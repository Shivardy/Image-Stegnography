Image Stegnography

Stegnography is the practice of concealing a file, message, image, or video within another file, message, image or video.

How to Use

Encryption:

Click on Encryption on the menu bar to encrypt a message inside an image, You need to enter a message and select an image before submitting for encryption.

Decryption:

Click on Decryption on the menu bar to decrypt a message from an image. You need to upload the image before submitting for decryption.
